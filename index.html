Students of School of the Art Insitute of Chicago's Writing for Film, Video, and Performance are pleased to present Expiration Date. Through experimental filmmaking, sound, and animation, this online screening explores the ups and downs of mental health, the beauty and horrors of dreamscapes, the lives of fictional characters, and the cycles of life and death.
